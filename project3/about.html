Gravity Art

Roll a ball to draw a generated shape. Press WASD keys to "tilt" the platform and roll the ball. Score higher the more accurate you are with the path.

  • Genre: Arcade
  • Story: Abstract
  • Aesthetics: Cartoony
  • Gameplay: Player has to think fast and adjust the tilt of their platform to make sure they don't get too far off course. There will be instructions to teach the player how to play.
