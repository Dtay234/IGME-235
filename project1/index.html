AquaSutra

Roles: Programming, Design, Art
Skills: Godot, GDScript

AquaSutra is a puzzle platformer where you play as a droplet of water trying to water a potted plant. Gather droplets from around the level, and use your abilities to shoot yourself and/or droplets out to complete the level!
